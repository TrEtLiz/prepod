# Feedback on Game Design Lectures

The material covers a wide range of topics - from basic concepts to specialized aspects such as character metrics and probability theory in games. ## Key Recommendations

### Missing Topics to Add:
1. **Narrative Design** - Storytelling, quests, dialogue systems
2. **Game Balancing** - Economy, difficulty curves, balance testing
3. **UI/UX Design** - Interface principles, accessibility
4. **Sound and Music** - Audio in gameplay, adaptive music
5. **Monetization** - F2P models, ethical considerations
6. **Analytics** - KPIs, A/B testing, retention metrics
7. **Prototyping** - Rapid prototyping methods, iterative design
8. **Multiplayer** - Social mechanics, community management

### Structural Improvements:
1. **Add a continuous project** - Develop a hypothetical game throughout the course
2. **Practical assignments** - 2-3 problems + 1 practical task per lecture
3. **Interactive elements** - More quizzes, video examples, GDC talk links
4. **Create a glossary** - Common terminology with cross-references
5. **Navigation** - Add Table of Contents and Previous/Next links

### Technical Enhancements:
1. **Create README.md** - Course description, structure, learning path
2. **Add metadata** - Estimated study time, difficulty level, prerequisites
3. **Standardize formatting** - Consistent heading styles, blockquotes, image captions
4. **Recommended resources** - Books, videos, tools for practice

Your lectures form a solid educational foundation for a comprehensive Game Design course. The material demonstrates deep subject understanding, good pedagogical preparation, and ability to structure complex information.
